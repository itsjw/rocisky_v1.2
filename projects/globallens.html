GlobalLens

A Visualization of Health Spending Factors

information visualization group project one month

Overview

In this information visualization project, we designed and implemented a new system GlobalLens that presents an evolution of various global indicators with both thematic map and relevance map. Our visualization system intends to aid users to understand specific indicators’ trends globally, and support researchers in terms of analyzing the correlation between different indicators. Users can choose the datasets they are interested in as the x-axis or y-axis, and explore their relevance with a time slider. We aim to support users to either explore specific countries’ data from a top-down process or discover any interesting findings with the help of synchronized world map view from a bottom-up process.
